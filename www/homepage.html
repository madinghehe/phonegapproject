Welcome!

Hi, my name is Ding Ma. I am studying informatics in Center for Urban Science & Progress (CUSP) of New York University. We CUSPers use New York City as our laboratory and classroom to help cities around the world become more productive, livable, equitable, and resilient. CUSP observes, analyzes, and models cities to optimize outcomes, prototype new solutions, formalize new tools and processes, and develop new expertise/experts.

My master program is based on knowledge in information technology and statistical analysis. I am familiar with SQL, Python, MATLAB and R. Having done many teamwork projects based on problems from city agencies, I am skilled to collect and clean data from diversified sources and validate raw data to make better-informed decisions after analysis.

As a person with curiosity, I am able to adapt to changing environments in a fast pace and get acquainted with new people and new skills quickly. Feel free to contact with me.
